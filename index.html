Disconnect Bluetooth & Wi-Fi in your Pocket

Pocket Mode protects your reproductive organs from exposure to radiation and heat. It smartly does it by disconnecting the Wi-fi and the Bluetooth, when in your pocket.


When you put a smartphone in your pocket, your body is being exposed to EMF (Electromotive force) Radiation. EMF in smartphones comes with non-ionized radiation and heat.

SAR is a measure of energy that is absorbed by human tissue. This energy comes in the form of radiation and heat

Radiation Bodyguard knows when your phone is in your pocket

Some of the radiation and heat comes from Bluetooth and Wi-Fi, which are continuously scanning and connecting to networks around you.


The networks say to each other: hi, what’s your name, protocols, etc. That’s why, when the setting is turned on, you can immediately see the available Wi-Fi and Bluetooth networks around you.

This is really unnecessary radiation and heat. You are anyway most likely walking in the street, and have no available Wi-Fi network.
It makes sense to avoid SAR radiation and heat so close to your reproductive organs.

When you turn on your phone’s screen or receive a call - immediately! Your Bluetooth and Wi-Fi will be turned on.

Phone Radiation Bodyguard App knows when your mobile phone is in your pocket, and then turns off the Wi-Fi and/or the Bluetooth, as you choose.


When you pull the smartphone out from your pocket - within 1 minute - your Bluetooth and Wi-Fi will be turned on.


Don’t worry about not being able to get phone calls or notifications. If your 'mobile data' is turned on (and if you have an internet plan with your sim card provider) you still have an internet connection while the mobile is in your pocket.


Time Reports for Pocket Mode: The app measures how long Pocket Mode is working. The app displays the number of hours, days, weeks, or even years you save from unnecessary Wifi & Bluetooth radiation exposure.

Phone Distance Trainer for Children

During a call, your phone emits more radiation. When holding your phone against your ear, there is no EMF protection between your cell phone and your brain.


HOW DOES IT WORK?

When the proximity sensor is activated during a call, a vibration warns your child to move the phone away from their head.


Over time, the app will train your child to keep a distance between the smartphone and their head and body.
